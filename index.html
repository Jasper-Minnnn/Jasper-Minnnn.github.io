Fanxu Min (闵凡煦)

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University, under the supervision of Prof. Wenkui Yu and Prof. Yinghuan Shi. I earned my M.S. in Software Engineering from Ocean University of China in 2025, advised by Prof. Junyu Dong. From 2024 to 2025, I visited Monash University/Airdoc as a research assistant, working with Prof. Zongyuan Ge.

My research interests center on harnessing state-of-the-art artificial-intelligence techniques to tackle clinical challenges and advance healthcare, especially in intensive care units (ICU) and emergency departments. Most recently, I have been focusing on deploying Multimodal (e.g. vision, language) LLM and Agent to support decision-making throughout the emergency-care workflow.
